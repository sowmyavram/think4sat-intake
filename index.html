Privacy & Data Use

This form collects the information you enter — academic background, interests, activities, and family/contact details — for one purpose only: to help Think4SAT build a personalized college advising plan for your student.

Submitted responses are sent directly to a private Google Sheet accessible only to Think4SAT staff. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with any third party, advertiser, or other organization.

While you're filling out the form, your in-progress answers are saved automatically in your own browser only (not sent anywhere) so an accidental refresh or closed tab doesn't erase your progress. That saved draft stays on your device and clears automatically once you submit, or any time you use "Clear form."

If you'd like your submitted information corrected or removed, contact us at info@think4sat.com or +1 (734) 972-3536.
